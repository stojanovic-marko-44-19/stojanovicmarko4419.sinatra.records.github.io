Production deal

If you are an independent artist with no experience in the music industry you can become our partner and through cooperation with us have access to everything necessary to make an album starting from pre-production through collaboration with songwriters, composers and arrangers through fundamental things for the production itself like recording studio, recording technicians, producers to necessities in post-production such as mixing and mastering of the material as well as recording sound on vinyls or CDs. If you are an establisted artist with a team you can become our partner just to the details of your interest. If you are interested in co-production cooperation you can define areas of cooperation with us.

Distribution deal

If you already have a finished album you can sign a cooperation agreement with us in the field of distribution. As our partner we can do the album marketing that suits you best. It may incude signing a contract with the TV company on your behalf on making TV spots and TV commercials for your album as well as on how to broadcast it. If you would like to focus the marketing of your album on the radio we can make a deal with the radio company on the making of a radio commercial for it, as well as on the frequency and timing of broadcasting on one or more radio stations. The deal also includes defining the retail price and defining the intermediaries.

Recording radio commercial

My Way deal

With this deal, you can complete the process of making an album within our company or create a cooperation agreement for defined areas of cooperation on both sides. The name of this deal suggests that you, as our partner, are not limited in any way in terms of everything necessary for the commercial success of your work of art, but that you can take only what is of interest to you from everything offered. It can be just marketing operations and the use of record studios or just collaboration in the field of physical distribution and post-production of albums or anything you can imagine. And that is not all. Like all other types of cooperation this deal also can be concluded on a long-term basis.

Rescue

Our support

If you are a promising artist our advise to you is to become our long-term partner. If an album is not recognized by critics or listeners which is reasonable to expect to happen once in a while in your career, it is important to have a our support. There are many performers who disappeared from the music scene after a couple of consecutive failures because record companies no longer wanted to take the risk of doing business with them. Our offer of a long-term contract to you is a confirmation of our trust in you and it gives you the opportunity to prove your quality and to rescue your career in case of failure. Also in the case of a distribution it is very important to you to have a reliable partner even after a certain failure.
