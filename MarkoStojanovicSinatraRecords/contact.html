Online contact procedure of Sinatra Records

The first thing you got to do is to fill out the contact form. You will be told if the particuler field is mandatory or not. Also you shall be told if you did not fill any particular field right. If you want to buy a specific album after selecting the name of any title you will be shown delivery costs by country in these United States as well as delivery costs worldwide. The costs are the same for all albums of all formats. After the sending within 48 hours to the e-mail address you left us you will receive a bill for the purchased album with the account number to which you need to pay for it or confirmation of a meeting with a representative of Sinatra Records in our building in Brooklyn at the chosen time if you want to become our partner. If you though want to complain about the lack of the product you need to select a specific defect. If the reason for sending your message is a compliment, suggestion or specific question we will be willing to thank you or respond with the desired information.
